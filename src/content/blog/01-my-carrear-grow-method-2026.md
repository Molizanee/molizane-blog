As an early career engineer, I'm always looking for ways to improve myself and my skills. I've been working as an engineer for about a year now, and I've learned a lot. But I know there's still so much more to learn. That's why I've decided to create this blog to document my journey and share my progress with you all. I'm hoping that by sharing my experiences, I can help other early career engineers who are just starting out. I'm also hoping that by sharing my progress, I can hold myself accountable and stay motivated. 

Here's my routine for 2026:

- 6:00 AM - Wake up and drink a glass of water
- 6:30 AM - Meditate for 10 minutes
- 6:40 AM - Read a book for 30 minutes
- 7:10 AM - Exercise for 30 minutes
- 7:40 AM - Shower and get ready for work
- 8:30 AM - Arrive at work and start working
- 12:00 PM - Lunch break
- 1:00 PM - Back to work
- 5:00 PM - Leave work
- 6:00 PM - Arrive home and relax
- 7:00 PM - Dinner
- 8:00 PM - Work on personal projects
- 10:00 PM - Read a book for 30 minutes
- 10:30 PM - Go to bed
